01 / About

Geodetic Engineer from Universitas Gadjah Mada.

I work across survey and mapping, GIS, remote sensing, geospatial data processing, 3D modeling for digital twin development, WebGIS development, and AI-driven spatial analysis. Throughout my academic and professional experiences, I have been involved in transforming aerial imagery, spatial datasets, and field-collected information into actionable geospatial products that support environmental monitoring, infrastructure management, and spatial decision-making.

My experience includes generating orthophotos and digital elevation models from aerial imagery, performing GIS-based spatial analysis, developing interactive WebGIS platforms, and creating detailed 3D models for digital twin applications. I have also worked with machine learning and AI-based approaches for spatial data analysis, particularly in environmental monitoring and geospatial research.

Currently, I am expanding my expertise through industry projects at Geo-AI Twinverse and applied research in geospatial technologies. I am passionate about integrating geospatial data, advanced analytics, and emerging technologies to develop innovative solutions for sustainable environmental management, infrastructure development, and digital transformation initiatives.
